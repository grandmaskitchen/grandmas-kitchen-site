Meet Grandma Lillian

Lillian was born in 1904 and lived to 104 years old. She grew up learning to cook from her mother and grandmother, survived two world wars, and raised a family through frugality, home-grown food, and traditional values.

This site is inspired by her legacy, to help modern families rediscover the timeless habits that truly nourish us.

“My Nan, Lillian, lived to 104 eating home-cooked meals every day. This site is my way of sharing her wisdom.” – Gary
